We are always welcoming new players and encourage people of all skill levels to give roller hockey a try.

For Experienced players: Our Division 2 team is highly competitive. Most players here have played hockey for many years at the junior or prep school level.

Just want to have fun: our Division 4 team is great for newer players or people looking to have fun. While still competitive, Division 4 accommodates all skill and interest levels.

How To Playmore_vert

closeHow To Play

Roller hockey and ice hockey are very similar - almost all of players grow up playing ice hockey. You will need roller skates, shin guards, elbow pads, gloves, a helmet, and a stick - we can help provide some equipment for new players.

The game is played 4 on 4 for three 12 minute periods. There are no offsides and no icing, so the game moves very quickly.
